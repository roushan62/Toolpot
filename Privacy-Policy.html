Toolpot Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we handle your information when you use our tools.

Information Collection

Toolpot is committed to protecting your privacy. Our tools are designed with the following principles:

  • We do not collect or store any personal information you enter into our tools
  • All processing happens locally in your browser
  • We do not use tracking cookies or analytics that identify individual users
  • Our server may log basic connection information (IP address, browser type) for security and maintenance

Data Security

Since all tool processing occurs in your browser:

  • Your data never leaves your device unless you choose to share it
  • We don't have servers that store your input or results
  • There's no risk of data breaches from our side

For maximum privacy, we recommend using our tools in private/incognito browsing mode if you're working with sensitive information.

Cookies

Toolpot uses minimal cookies:

  • Optional preference cookies to remember your settings (if any tool has configurable options)
  • No advertising or tracking cookies
  • You can disable cookies in your browser settings without affecting tool functionality

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external sites:

  • We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of these sites
  • We recommend reviewing the privacy policy of any site you visit

Secure & Private
